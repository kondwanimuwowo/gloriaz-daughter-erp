3. Order Management

Step-by-Step Order Creation

  1. Click + Create Order in the sidebar or dashboard.
  2. Select the Order Type (Custom or Pre-designed).
  3. Search for an existing customer or add a new one instantly.
  4. Select materials and specify quantities.
  5. Review the auto-calculated costs and set your final selling price.
  6. Log the deposit paid to generate the balance due.

4. Customer Management

Keep a detailed database of every client, including their unique measurements and order history.

  • Measurements: Store values like Bust, Waist, Hips, and Shoulder width for repeat orders.
  • Order History: Access all past invoices and designs for specific customers.
  • Profiles: Update contact info and delivery addresses easily.

5. Inventory Management

Track your fabrics, threads, and finished goods in real-time.

  • Stock Alerts: System alerts appear in red when a material is running low.
  • Restocking: Use the 'Restock' action to update stock and average cost.
  • Usage: Inventory is automatically deducted when you create custom or pre-designed orders.
