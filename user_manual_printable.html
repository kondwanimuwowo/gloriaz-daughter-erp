4. Customer Management

Track customer profiles, measurements, and order history in one place.

  • Profiles: Store phone numbers, emails, and notes.
  • Measurements: Save detailed measurements for repeat clients.
  • History: See every order a customer has placed.

5. Inventory Management

Manage your fabrics, trims, and finished goods.

  • Real-time Tracking: Stock levels update automatically as you use materials in orders.
  • Restocking: Log new purchases to update your average cost per unit.
  • Low Stock Alerts: Set thresholds to get notified before you run out.

Access professional reports to see how your business is performing.
